02 / About

Curiosity,
under timing constraints.

I’m an Electrical Engineering undergraduate who likes the point where clean logic meets physical reality.

During a DRDO-funded research internship at IIT Jodhpur, I independently brought up two FPGA SoC systems on real hardware. I’m pursuing a career in VLSI verification, and I care about the details that make designs trustworthy: clock-domain crossings, reset behavior, assertions, coverage, timing, and clear documentation.

Right now I’m expanding into UVM, cocotb, and embedded Linux on FPGA.
